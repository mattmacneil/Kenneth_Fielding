About KFCM:

Starting his career in 2000, Kenneth has continually refined his process, while fixing his eyes on the future of the beauty industry. His keen eye for detail and love of fashion is evident in his body of work. He showcases beautiful, soft, natural looks alongside bright and bold showing his creative versatility all the while maintaining the health and integrity of your hair.
